Race 4 Space: online version of the classic game

Are you looking for fun?

Race 4 Space is a classic game also known as Four in a Row or Connect4. It is popular around whole world. That might be because of its simplicity. You can teach any child how to play in just few seconds. Perfect for family game sessions. It is also very good brain exercise. Connect 4 teaches logical thinking. If you want to be successful player you need to be patient and predict future moves.
